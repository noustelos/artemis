Why Dogs Bark Excessively

Barking is often triggered by alertness, frustration, anxiety, or a lack of predictability in the environment. When owners respond only to the noise, they miss the underlying emotion. Lasting change usually comes from reducing stressors, teaching alternative behaviors, and helping the dog feel safer and more understood.
